Backtest fidelity

5 of 43 resolved trades (12%) exited on the fill bar — the same H1 candle that filled the limit also hit SL (and sometimes TP1). Read the headline knowing this slice is H1-resolution-limited, not clean directional losses — a lower-timeframe fill model would reclassify some.
