Backtest fidelity — same-bar resolutions

8 of 59 resolved trades (14%) exited on the fill bar — the same H1 candle that filled the limit also hit SL (and sometimes TP1). On H1 we cannot tell whether TP or SL printed first, so these are booked SL (pessimistic, no look-ahead). Of the losers, 2 had TP1's price touched in-bar yet booked a full −1R. Read the headline knowing this slice is H1-resolution-limited, not clean directional losses — a lower-timeframe fill model would reclassify some.

How far every trade ran in your favour (MFE) and against you (MAE) before it resolved. This is the direct read on whether the stop is too tight, the target too near, or a break-even rule is overdue.
