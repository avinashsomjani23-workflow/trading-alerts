Act 6 · Can I trust this run

The honesty checks: did the gates pass, where did trades drop out, and what the H1 resolution can and can't tell you.

Gates

✓ All 10 integrity gates passed.

Audit-only (never in P&L): never-filled 56, timeout 2, data-window-end 0, IST-blocked 0. Standing policy: unresolved positions never feed expectancy.

Backtest fidelity

42 of 209 resolved trades (20%) exited on the fill bar — the same H1 candle that filled the limit also hit SL (and sometimes TP1). On H1 we cannot tell whether TP or SL printed first, so these are booked SL (pessimistic, no look-ahead). Of the losers, 0 had TP1's price touched in-bar yet booked a full −1R. Read the headline knowing this slice is H1-resolution-limited, not clean directional losses — a lower-timeframe fill model would reclassify some.

Validation guard: no filter (news / killzone) excluded any trade from P&L this period — both are display-only as intended.
